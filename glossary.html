GD3 Glossary of Terms

Animatic

taking keyframes from the storyboard and editing and displaying them in sequence with a rough dialogue and/or rough sound track to test whether the sound and images are working effectively together

Blocking

putting in basic timing and poses to communicate where one is going with an animated scene

Narrative

an acount; a tale; a story

Temporal

of, pertaining to, limited by, or generally dealing with time
